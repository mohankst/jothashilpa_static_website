Welcome

Jothashilpa - a centre for traditional and contemporary arts, has started its journey for enhancement of arts practices in Bangladesh. It juxtaposes various categories of arts to create a new art language rooted in this land.



Research & publications

Research and Publications wing undertakes and disseminates research on various topics of culture and arts (both traditional and contemporary). We have a team of experienced researchers and analysts with proven track record. We also provide professional manuscript development, book design and printing services.

Crafts

Crafts wing produces wide ranges of uniquely designed crafts items for tasteful lifestyle consumption. Design, development and supply chain management of crafts items is executed by veteran designers, artists and craftsmen. We also help clients in developing customized craft products for all occasions.

Event and workshops

Events and Workshops are led by Jothashilpa’s operations team. We have a pool of knowledgeable resource persons, skillful event managers and strong networks of vendors. With our leadership and these resources we have successfully organized events and workshop in many venues across Bangladesh.

Training Programs

Training Programs wing organizes various capacity development and continued professional development trainings focused on arts, fashion designing and crafts making. Credentials are also awarded to the successful participants of Jothashilpa organized training programs.
